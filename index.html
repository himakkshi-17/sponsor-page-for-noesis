NOESIS 6.0

noesis 6.0

Noesis is an annual mega event of VISION,the technical society of NIT-Bhopal. Started in 2011,it challenges engineering students across domains every year. We are continually faced by great opportunities brilliantly disguised as insoluble problems. With that thought in mind, Vision, announces the 6th edition of NOESIS, a platform which is yet again going to challenge your engineering skills across various domains. Only this time, it's going to be harder than what you have seen.

WORKSHOPS

BYTE THE CODE

Byte the code is a workshop on python. In this workshop we aim to introduce students to main structure and working of Python. This year it witnessed more than 400 participations. The highlight of event was a project on web scrapping. To wrap things up there was a quiz which tested coding skills and intellect of students, and winners were awarded.

ASPHEN-TECH

One of the workshops in Noesis, ASPEN-TECH is being organized every year. It's a chemical engineering software but anyone who is interested to learn, can attend the event. The event is totally student interactive.

AAKRITI

AAKRITI, a workshop organised to shape and mould the foundations of newly generated buds of CIVIL ENGINEERING. Its main focus was on creating an interactive session to enlighten Civil Engineering students about their branch and open up the new doors of excellence.
